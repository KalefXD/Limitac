Limitac - Tic Tac Toe with limited rules

Options
  • Version Spanish
Player X's turn

Instructions:

  • Make a row, column or diagonal of the same mark to win.
  • Three marks per player; placing a fourth mark removes the oldest mark, indicated by the lowest number.
  • (In development) If you take too long to place the mark, it will be placed in a random space.
  • You can only place one mark around the previous mark; if there is no room, the surrounding marks will be deleted.
